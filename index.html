Career Programs

Healthcare & community programs with real career outcomes

Short, in-demand programs that lead to stable jobs across Ontario — chosen with you, not for a commission.

High demand

Personal Support Worker (PSW)

A 6–8 month diploma that opens doors in long-term care, retirement homes and community settings — one of Ontario's fastest paths to a stable healthcare role.

  • Anatomy, infection control, cognitive impairments
  • Practical placement at certified facilities
  • Earn while you learn after graduation
Career Step: Bridge to Practical Nursing or Nursing Assistant with extra training.
Family friendly

Early Childhood Education Assistant (ECEA)

Train to support licensed childcare centres — a stable career with steady demand for parents returning to work or new graduates.

  • Child development, play-based learning, safety
  • Bridge to full ECE certification
  • Roles across schools, daycares & before/after-school programs
Career Step: Stackable credentials toward Registered Early Childhood Educator (RECE).
Quick start

Pharmacy Assistant

A focused certificate that prepares you for retail and hospital pharmacy support — strong starter role with progression into pharmacy technician.

  • Prescription processing, inventory, customer care
  • Practicum at major Canadian pharmacy chains
  • Steady weekday hours, predictable income
Career Step: Bridge to Regulated Pharmacy Technician with extra training.
Meaningful

Community Support Worker (CSW)

Support adults with developmental disabilities, mental health needs or addictions across Ontario — one of the most rewarding social-service roles.

  • Behavioural support, crisis response, advocacy
  • Government and not-for-profit employers
  • Pathway to social work and counselling careers
Career Step: Stackable credentials toward Social Service Worker (SSW) diploma.
